Comet.io - Technical Documentation

Pass by spots and plan your next event

Comet is a geo-based marketing network that will allow users to pass by next weekend destinations unknowingly


The big idea: Comet sets out to solve the hardest problem that plaques the world - finding new stuff to do.

What is comet?

Finding new events often happens by accident or through word-of-mouth. When we purposefully look for our next weekend plan it often means glancing through websites or social media pages mindlessly.

By utilizing the future of advanced GPS, passing by buildings can uncover your next amazing hangout. Businesses can now turn ice-cold leads into recurring customers. Awareness is the hardest part of the sales funnel and now it could be automated.

Through the power of the network effect, businesses and customers can become interconnected and accurately matched through algorithms and AI systems.

Comet App Users

Users can join for free by downloading the app, signing up, and creating a profile. Users will be asked about their interests and habits; this data is then used to create a user avatar for the sytem to leverage when trying to match users with event ideas.

Comet Business

Business can leverage comet by joining our network and thus becoming discoverable locally and internationally.

As local customers walk past your business our network collects data and leverages it to connect your business with customers that aren't so local.

This business data is ever evolving and compounds as the network grows. And with the addition of advertisements, content-marketing, and a constant stream of exposure to potential customers through our reccomendation system.

Comet Network

Main data points are:

  1. Brands
  2. GPS Spots
  3. Customers

It's a simple network and when any one data point increases in number it improves the others and further raises the number of the other points.

Comet & Starlink

With the advent of Starlink, Comet will be able to utilize the GPS to accurately measure the physical interactions that brands and customers have with one another.

5G and increasing internet speeds all-around will make connections between all these data points possible.

Registration

Users can download the app and create an account for free immediantly while businesses can also do the same for free.

After a few months, business will pay $5 a month to stay on the platform if they choose and it will theoretically give them and easy ROI.

Event History & Future Events

Brands will be set up with a profile that will showcase previous events that they've hosted and have a calendar that shows their future events, too.

Organic content can be used for reccomendations by the system and advertisements for paid traffic.

Algorithm: Local to International

Based on what users like to do in their local setting (and how they interact with their local setting), that data could be projected on to an international setting; wherever the user goes, fun could be found for them.

Monetization

Users use the app for free as they are the potential customers likely visiting these businesses. However, brands pay $5 to stay on the map and more if they're actively advertising certain ticket events.
